" these new findings are significant because they show that our technique is effective in finding ice when it is on the surface ," says professor michael a ' hearn of the university of maryland , the project ' s principal investigator . " we can [ also ] therefore firmly conclude that most of the water vapor that escapes from comets is contained in ice particles found below the surface ." into the heart of a comet deep impact slammed
