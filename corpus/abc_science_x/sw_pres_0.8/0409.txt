" teaching is ubiquitous in human societies , but although social learning is widespread in other species , it is not yet clear how commonly teaching is involved ," the researchers write . the meerkats demonstrate clearly that they teach , and do not merely allow the pups to learn by observing , thornton and mcauliffe say . " a greater understanding of the evolution of teaching is essential if we are to further our knowledge of human cultural evolution and for us to examine the relations between culture in our own
